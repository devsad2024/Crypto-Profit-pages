CryptoProfit

BTC Portfolio Tracker

A mobile app for tracking Bitcoin purchases, deposits, and portfolio profit/loss over time, with optional read-only sync from Bybit. Available on iOS and Android.

Features

  • Deposits — log a pending deposit, then convert it into a BTC purchase at the historical price for that date
  • Purchases — record BTC buys, edit or delete unsold entries, mark a purchase as sold to lock in realized P&L
  • Bybit sync — optionally connect a read-only Bybit API key to import your real deposit and trade history
  • Live & historical BTC price lookups
  • Portfolio dashboard — overall P&L, total invested, total BTC, average BTC purchase price
  • P&L history chart — interactive chart of portfolio value and profit/loss over time, in USD or ILS

How it works

  • Log a deposit, or import your history via Bybit sync
  • Convert a pending deposit into a BTC purchase — the BTC price is fetched automatically for that date
  • Mark a purchase as sold to lock in realized P&L at the sale-date price; unsold purchases track live unrealized P&L
  • View the P&L history screen for a full timeline of portfolio value and profit/loss
